About Me


Hello! I’m Cristina, a passionate web developer with a love for creating interactive, user-friendly websites. My journey began with a curiosity for how websites are built, which soon grew into a dedication to mastering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and beyond. I enjoy bringing ideas to life online, crafting experiences that are both visually engaging and technically robust. Whether working on personal projects or collaborating with others, I’m always excited to learn, innovate, and tackle new challenges in the world of web development. When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me exploring new tech trends or experimenting with design ideas. Thanks for stopping by, and feel free to check out my projects!

Career Goals


As I continue developing my skills in web development, I’m focused on a set of goals to guide my growth and impact in the field:


1. Master Full-Stack Development


I aim to deepen my understanding of both front-end and back-end technologies, building a solid foundation in full-stack development. This includes improving my skills in frameworks like React and Node.js.


2. Build a Portfolio of Meaningful Projects


My goal is to work on projects that are not only technically challenging but also impactful. I’m particularly interested in developing applications that solve real-world problems and enhance user experiences.


3. Contribute to Open Source


Giving back to the developer community is important to me. I plan to contribute to open-source projects, where I can learn from experienced developers while sharing my own knowledge and skills.


4. Stay Updated with Emerging Technologies


Technology is always evolving, and I’m committed to staying informed on new tools and practices. I’m particularly excited to explore areas like AI, machine learning, and responsive design to bring innovative solutions to my work.


5. Mentor and Collaborate


I believe in the power of collaboration and knowledge-sharing. As I grow in my career, I hope to mentor others, contribute to a supportive developer community, and build a network of inspiring collaborators.
